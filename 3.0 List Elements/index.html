Cinamon rolls recipe

Ingredients

For the dough

  • 3/4 cup of warm milk
  • 2 1/4 teaspoon yeast
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg + 1 egg yolk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 3 cup bread flour

For the filling

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 1/" tablespoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup butter

Instructions

  1. Mix the milk with the yeast, sugar, eggs.
  2. Melt the butter and add to the mixture.
  3. Add in the flour and mix until combined into a dough.
  4. Knead the dough for 10 minutes.
  5. Transfer the dough into a large bowl and cover with plastic wrap.
    Leave it somewhere to ise for 2 hours.
  6. After the dough has doubled in size, roll it out into a rectangle.
